Robot Wars Competition

Get ready for the ultimate robotics showdown! Our annual Robot Wars competition features two exciting categories.

üõ°Ô∏è Non-Destructive Battle

The Strategy Challenge

  • Objective: Push your opponent's robot out of the circular arena boundary
  • Arena: Fixed circular boundary marked clearly on the ground
  • No Damage Allowed: Robots must not cause any physical damage to opponents
  • Pushing Only: Victory achieved through strategic positioning and pushing power
  • Match Duration: 3 minutes per round
  • Disqualification: Any intentional damage results in immediate disqualification
  • Weight Limit: Maximum 15kg per robot
  • Victory Conditions: Robot pushed completely outside the boundary or immobilized for 10 seconds

‚öîÔ∏è Destructive Battle

The Ultimate Showdown

  • Objective: Push your opponent out of the circular arena by any means necessary
  • Arena: Same fixed circular boundary as non-destructive category
  • Damage Allowed: All forms of mechanical damage are permitted
  • Fire Prohibited: Flame weapons, incendiary devices, and fire-based attacks are strictly forbidden
  • Match Duration: 5 minutes per round
  • Weight Limit: Maximum 25kg per robot
  • Weapons Allowed: Spinning blades, hammers, flippers, crushers, saws (no flames)
  • Victory Conditions: Opponent pushed out, immobilized for 10 seconds, or disabled
  • Safety: All robots must have emergency stop mechanisms
